Fix: Add getAll() methods to products, materials, machines, colors, and suppliers in db.js

The AppContext.jsx and SuppliersPage.jsx were calling getAll() methods that did not exist in db.js.
These entities only had list(userId) methods. Added getAll() async methods for these entities that
leverage Supabase RLS policies (user_id = auth.uid()) to automatically filter results by the
authenticated user without requiring userId parameter.

- products.getAll()
- materials.getAll()
- machines.getAll()
- colors.getAll()
- suppliers.getAll()

This fixes the critical bug preventing the app from loading master data at startup.
